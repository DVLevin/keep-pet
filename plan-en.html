§ 1The Promise

What thirty thousand euros actually buys.

A working MVP across two surfaces — Telegram Mini App + PWA — shipping the full Apr-21-locked product spine. Software-only. Two rails. Same brain, two doors.

In scope — v1, software-only
  • Brain + Passport — onboarding via OCR + MCQ + voice in 10–15 minutes; persistent per-pet knowledge graph; daily proactive check-ins
  • Voice-first interaction — sub-second response, conversational not transactional
  • Vet voice-memo capture— Yulia, Apr 21 — record vet → translate → routine extraction → memory ingestion
  • QR-shareable read-only passport view — one tap, any vet, any language
  • Lost-pet poster generator— Yulia, Apr 21 — auto-flyer for Telegram / WhatsApp / Instagram
  • Subscription gate at $3 / mo, framed as "infrastructure support / early adopter"
  • Phase-1 success instrumentation — retention, LTV, inference-cost telemetry from day one
Out of v1 — deliberate, per Apr 21 lock
  • Wearable hardware ingestion— Yulia blocked v1; v3 lane, contingent
  • Native iOS / Android app— PWA covers until limits bite
  • Smart-speaker integration— v3 lane, gated on engagement metrics
  • Pet emotion estimation feature— v3 lane; depends on wearable lane reopening + safe-claim envelope (RR #14)
  • Russia-territory entity / brand— separate workstream, separate cap-table firewall
