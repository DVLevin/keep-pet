Keep Pet — the place where your pet is already known.

No one knows your pet but you. Keep Pet is the answer to the question every vet, every petsitter, every concerned owner asks at 2 a.m.: what's normal for this animal?

The full canonical version is in Russian. Browser translation (Chrome, Safari) handles it well. The summary below is for fast orientation.

01The wedge

Cats first. 15.9M cats in Germany (+18% 2021–24). Cat owners are underserved by every existing pet-app on the market — they all default to dogs. Dogs are the workhorse market; cats are how we win.

02The MLP

One brain, several doors. TMA + PWA for v1. Smart speakers not first version — gated by D30 ≥ 25% retention + 15% voice usage. The biggest honest shift from v1 was acknowledging that voice-everywhere is the north star, but voice-first is not the right starting product.

03Core MLP features (Yulia, Apr 21)

  • Vet voice-memo — owner records vet visit; AI transcribes + extracts diagnosis, meds, follow-ups; lives forever in the pet's passport.
  • Lost-pet poster — one tap → printable + shareable poster with QR linking to the live passport.
  • Live passport — QR-shareable, auto-syncing record. Vet visits, weight, photos, behavior notes, breed quirks. Belongs to the pet, not the platform.
  • Pet-emotion estimation — v3 lane, biometric-only (heart rate, posture). Never "translator" — Traini-style claims under legal exposure. Safe-claim envelope ready; ships post Phase-2 raise + wearable reopens.

04Geography

v1 polygon: Germany #1 (defensible — diaspora research #10, pet-population research #11). Israel was v1 candidate but fell to #2. Italy in background as Tier-2 cats-first signal — third polygon candidate for v6.

05Cap table

HQ in Tier-1 jurisdiction (Delaware / UK / NL). Not Cyprus. Russia cap-table treatment per 15+ post-2022 precedents — research brief #13.

06What's locked, what's open

  • Locked: cats-first wedge · TMA+PWA MLP · Germany v1 polygon · vet voice-memo + lost-pet poster as core features · pet-emotion as v3 biometric-only · Tier-1 HQ.
  • Open (this review window): roadmap pacing · Iteration 1 budget · team allocation · Phase-2 raise timing.
